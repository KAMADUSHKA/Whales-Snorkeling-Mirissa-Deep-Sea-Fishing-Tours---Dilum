Deep Sea Fishing Tours in Mirissa


Reel in the Adventure – Far Beyond the Shoreline


Take your fishing experience to the next level with our Deep Sea Fishing Tour in the stunning waters of the Indian Ocean. Ideal for beginners and seasoned anglers alike, this tour promises action, relaxation, and unforgettable ocean views.

  • What’s Included :

  • ✔ Free Pickup & Drop-off (within the Mirissa area)
  • ✔ All Fishing Gear & Equipment Provided
  • ✔ Snacks & Delicious Food Onboard
  • ✔ Life Jackets & Full Safety Gear Provided
  • ✔ Experienced Local Crew & Fishing Guide

With safety as our top priority, all tours are led by experienced professionals and equipped with reliable gear. Enjoy your time on the open sea with full support from our friendly and knowledgeable team.

What You Can Catch:

Venture into deeper waters where you’ll have the chance to catch a variety of prized species, including:

  • Tuna
  • Bonito
  • Mackerel
  • Other Seasonal Game Fish

Whether you're after the thrill of the chase or a relaxing day at sea, this tour delivers the perfect balance of both.
